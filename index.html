About Me

A Eye for the
Unseen Moment

I'm a photographer based in New York, working across editorial, commercial, and fine-art photography. My work is rooted in observation — finding the overlooked, the transient, the exact second before everything changes.

With over seven years of experience shooting for global brands, galleries, and private clients, I bring a quiet intensity to every frame.
